What I've done so far

On this first floor is the majority of things I have created on my own, my own inspiration, my own creations, I'm proud of my work but I have a long way to go to become amazing at what I create. Just like every other floor it starts with floor 1 and the rest come after.

I challenge myself to create all 64 Floors and watch myself and everyone around me grow as I go.
