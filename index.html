What is 64 Floors

The whole idea behind 64 Floors is 'leveling up'. Each project that I work on will be its own Floor. The symolism of walking through the building and to see me grow and develop as I practice and create more and more content. You need the frist floor to get to the second and they all add on top of each other. I want to push my personal boundries and do things I would never expect to do.

I'd doing this because I was working a 9-5 Job that left me feeling drained every single day. I wanted to see myself develop as I did enjoy doing creative things. So follow me along and watch my art and myself grow along the way!
