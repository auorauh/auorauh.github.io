What is 64 Floors

The whole idea behind 64 Floors is ‘leveling up.’ Each floor in the building will portray a different creative project that I have been working on. I see myself walking through this building, watching myself grow and develop creatively as I make more and more content. The idea is that you need the first floor to get to the second, and the floors build on each other. Eventually, I will have so many creative projects where I will have pushed my boundaries and do things I would never expect to do. Whether that’s creating a website, doing a choreographed dance, or so many other exciting things I have coming.

The reason I started 64 Floors is because I was working a 9-5 job that was leaving me unfulfilled. I wanted to see myself do something meaningful, creative, and just watch myself grow. I love being creative, it’s something that truly makes me happy in life. So follow me along the way and watch my art and myself develop!
